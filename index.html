Red root floaters(Phyllanthus Fluitans)

Red Root Floaters, or Phyllanthus Fluitans, are a freshwater floating fern plant .Its Easy to grow . Under right conditions the leaves turn slightly reddish and flowers. Originated from the American tropics

Water Cabbage(Pistia stratiotes)

Water lettuce is a floating aquatic plant.It is often called water cabbage, water lettuce, Nile cabbage, or shellflower.Its roots hang inside water beneath floating leaves.

Red Cabomba(Cabomba furcata)

Red Cabomba is a densely growing plant that makes an excellent, natural background in a planted aquarium.They are pretty hardy and can handle varying conditions quite well.

Green Cabomba(Cabomba caroliniana)

Cabomba caroliniana is an aquatic perennial herbaceous plant native to North and South America.It's a very beautiful aquarium plant. It doesn't require high light or special care.Green cabomba grow very fast in all conditions.To propagate new plant cut the stem and put it in the substrate and it will grow as a new plant..

Amazon Frogbit( limnobium laevigatum)

Amazon frogbit is a freshwater aquarium plant common among aquarists that prefer rearing plants in their home aquariums.They can absorb the harmful nutrition thus controlling the algae and improving water quality

Green Foxtail(Ceratophyllum demersum)

'Foxtail' is also popular as Hornwort. It is a popular freshwater and unique aquarium plant that grows very fast.
